The GDV-library tries to provide a simple data-visualization-library in the gtk+-framework. It is the attempt to use the powerful programming-features of the glib- and gtk+-libraries such as object-oriented programming, theme-settings by CSS-files, referencing, introspection and widget-based geometry management in a professional data-visualization-library. It works on many UNIX-like platforms, as well as Windows and OS X. LibGDV is released under the terms and conditions of the GNU Lesser General Public License (GNU LGPL).
